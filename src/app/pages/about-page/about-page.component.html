Hi,

My name is João Alexandre Carvalho Marinho dos Santos and I'm 23 years old.

I'm currently finishing my Master's degree in Informatics and Computing Engineering in FEUP, on the 5th year of studies.

In the past year I've been to Rzeszow, in Poland, on Erasmus+ programme where I stayed for a whole semester at UITM (University of Information Technology and Management) with a global grade of 18,75.

Since my early ages, I developed a big interest in videogames, and that lead me to this course. Alongside my academic studies, I had the chance to learn many game engines such as Unity, Unreal Engine, libGDX that I have used to create many schoolar projects.

My areas of interest are:
  • Game Development
  • Augmented/Virtual Reality
  • Web and App Development, more related to Back-End
  • Software Development and System Design
  • Database Administration
  • Big Data

Besides having these preferences, I am open to other topics as well.

I'm interested and keen to learn new things and flexible in terms of working in almost any type of topic.

I'm a good team worker, very organized, hardworker, optimist, responsable and I can easily adaptat to any work environment.
